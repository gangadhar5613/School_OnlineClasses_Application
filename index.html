About

Sree Nalanda High School was established in June 2008. With a strength of 123 students upto 5th class. We got efficient teaching staff,with their help the school strength is now raised upto nearly 1000 students upto 10th class. The school is located in Eguvapet,Siddavatam.In an extent of 15,700 sq.feet

Vision

The most important goal of Sree Nalanda High School is to impart quality education that will nurture academic excellence of the highest class. To make every student self-confident of their knowledge and skills what they are gaining through our curriculam

Objectives

The school focuses on overall development of student. The school will offer true education that builds courage instill self confidence,values and an understanding of rights & duties. Inculcating the scientific temper among the students.Conducting regular competetions in academic and extra circular activities
